Selected Projects and Strategic Work

INTERLAD’s project experience reflects our commercially versatile approach across logistics, procurement, consulting, strategic development and renewable energy. The examples below highlight selected assignments and areas of involvement across Africa. Certain details have been adapted or presented in summary form in order to respect client confidentiality and commercial sensitivity.

Public Sector Procurement

Borehole drilling equipment supply, installation and maintenance in Lualaba, DRC

INTERLAD supported the procurement and delivery of borehole drilling equipment for the public sector in Lualaba, Democratic Republic of the Congo. Our role included sourcing coordination, logistics support and follow-through on implementation requirements.

Campaign and Industrial Supply

Printing equipment, factories and campaign material supply

INTERLAD coordinated the supply of campaign-related equipment including large-format printing equipment, two complete printing factories and 2,000,000+ T-shirts for political stakeholders in the DRC. We also provided guidance on installation, after-sales support and warranties.

Market Development and Fintech Structuring

Market research and business development across West and North Africa

INTERLAD conducted market research and business development support for a private equity firm across Algeria, Nigeria, Burkina Faso, Mali, Chad, Equatorial Guinea and Egypt. This work contributed to the structuring of a remittance model that enabled payments into and out of Chad through USSD code integration and local partnerships.

Renewable Energy Development | DRC

Uvira renewable energy initiative

INTERLAD was involved in renewable energy development work in Uvira, supporting project planning and coordination efforts around energy solutions in the region. Although the initiative could not progress as intended due to the security situation and conflict-related disruption, the project reflected our active role in complex energy opportunities in challenging environments.

Consulting | Feasibility Studies

Financial and technical feasibility studies for solar and strategic projects

INTERLAD has undertaken financial and technical feasibility studies for solar power projects and other strategic ventures, supporting clients with project evaluation, commercial structuring, technical review, implementation assumptions and decision-making inputs. This consulting capability forms a core part of our value proposition across energy and investment-linked opportunities.

Logistics | Pan-African Coordination

Air, sea and road freight coordination across Africa

INTERLAD has coordinated airfreight, sea freight and road freight movements across multiple African markets, supporting clients with shipment planning, supplier coordination, routing support, cargo follow-through and cross-border delivery requirements. This work reflects our practical logistics capability across varied cargo profiles and operating environments.

Renewable Energy | Copperbelt Region

Renewable energy projects in Katanga, DRC

INTERLAD is actively involved in major renewable energy initiatives in the Copperbelt region of the Democratic Republic of the Congo, including utility-scale and commercial-energy opportunities linked to power access, industrial demand and long-term infrastructure development. These projects reflect our growing role in bankable energy development and strategic project support in high-potential markets.

Renewable Energy | ECOWAS and CEMAC Region

Strategic renewable energy development across ECOWAS and CEMAC

INTERLAD is also engaged in significant renewable energy opportunities in ECOWAS and CEMAC, supporting project development efforts in a market where reliable power infrastructure remains critical to industrial and economic growth. Our involvement reflects a broader regional commitment to energy transition, infrastructure delivery and commercially viable project structuring.

Public Sector and Industrial Supply

Procurement, delivery and implementation support across multiple sectors

Beyond consulting and renewable energy, INTERLAD has delivered procurement and supply assignments across public-sector, industrial and commercial environments, combining sourcing, logistics coordination, delivery support, installation guidance and after-sales follow-through. Our project history demonstrates versatility, responsiveness and an ability to execute across diverse mandates.
