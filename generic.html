About INTERLAD

The International Logistics and Distribution Company (Pty) Ltd is a commercially driven business platform operating across logistics, procurement, distribution, consulting, project support and strategic ventures across Africa.

Who We Are

INTERLAD is an opportunity-driven company built around practical execution, trusted coordination and commercial adaptability. We support clients across sectors by helping them source, move, structure and deliver solutions in environments where experience, flexibility and follow-through matter.

Our work spans logistics, procurement, business development, consulting support, renewable energy initiatives and special projects. Rather than being limited to one narrow line of activity, INTERLAD is structured to identify legitimate opportunities, understand operational realities and create value through execution.

What We Stand For

We believe business growth comes from solving real problems, entering viable markets and maintaining the flexibility to pursue new opportunities responsibly. INTERLAD combines entrepreneurial thinking with a practical understanding of cross-border trade, supplier coordination, freight movements, project requirements and regional market dynamics.

Our approach is grounded in professionalism, responsiveness and long-term relationship building. Whether supporting a procurement mandate, coordinating freight, contributing to a feasibility study or helping shape a strategic project, we aim to bring clarity, momentum and commercially relevant support to every engagement.

Our Vision

Our vision is to grow INTERLAD into a trusted African business platform recognised for execution, versatility and strategic value creation across multiple industries. We aim to expand our impact through partnerships, project development, consulting support and commercially sound ventures that contribute to business growth and regional development.

As we continue to evolve, we remain focused on legitimate opportunity, practical delivery and building a business that can move confidently across sectors, borders and emerging markets.
