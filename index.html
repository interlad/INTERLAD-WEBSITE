About INTERLAD

The International Logistics and Distribution Company (Pty) Ltd is a commercially driven and opportunity-focused business platform operating across logistics, procurement, distribution, consulting, project support and strategic ventures across Africa. We work across sectors and borders, helping clients move from concept to execution through practical coordination, versatility and value-focused delivery.

Who We Are

Commercially versatile and execution-led

INTERLAD is built to operate in dynamic and often complex business environments where clients need more than a narrowly defined service provider. We identify opportunities, coordinate stakeholders, support procurement and delivery, and help structure practical solutions across trade, logistics, energy, consulting and strategic ventures. Our strength lies in adaptability, execution and a willingness to take on legitimate new opportunities where value can be created through focused solutions to client challenges.

What We Do

Supporting growth and creating value across sectors

From sourcing and cross-border logistics to renewable energy, water treatment, consulting, market development, fintech-related initiatives and special projects, INTERLAD helps organisations navigate complexity and pursue opportunities with confidence. We combine practical coordination with entrepreneurial thinking to deliver solutions that are commercially relevant, operationally grounded and adaptable to different environments.
